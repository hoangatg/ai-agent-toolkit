# Copywriting

> Words that convert readers into users, users into customers.

---

## 1. Copywriting Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Benefit over feature** | "Save 10 hours/week" not "Automated scheduler" |
| **Clear over clever** | Clarity beats creativity |
| **Specific over vague** | "10,000+ users" not "Many users" |
| **Active voice** | "We build" not "It is built by" |
| **Scannable** | Headers, bullets, short paragraphs |

---

## 2. Landing Page Framework

### AIDA Structure

| Section | Purpose | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **Attention** | Hook with pain point | "Tired of losing leads?" |
| **Interest** | Show you understand | "We know sales teams waste 5h/day on..." |
| **Desire** | Show the solution | "Automate follow-ups, close 3x more" |
| **Action** | Clear CTA | "Start free trial →" |

### Above the Fold

| Element | Must Have |
|---------|----------|
| **Headline** | Clear value proposition |
| **Subheadline** | Support with detail |
| **CTA** | One primary action |
| **Social proof** | Logos, numbers, or testimonial |
| **Visual** | Product screenshot or demo |

---

## 3. CTA Design

### CTA Principles

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Action-oriented** | "Start building" not "Submit" |
| **Value-focused** | "Get my free report" not "Download" |
| **Urgency** | "Limited spots" or "Today only" |
| **One CTA per section** | Don't overwhelm with choices |

---

## 4. Email Sequences

### Welcome Sequence (5 emails)

| Email | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1. Welcome | Immediately | Confirm, set expectations |
| 2. Quick win | Day 1 | First value delivered |
| 3. Social proof | Day 3 | Case study or testimonial |
| 4. Deep dive | Day 5 | Feature education |
| 5. Activation | Day 7 | CTA to upgrade/complete setup |

---

## 5. SEO Content

| Principle | Application |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Search intent** | Match what users actually search for |
| **Headers hierarchy** | H1 → H2 → H3, keyword-rich |
| **Meta description** | 150-160 chars, include CTA |
| **Internal linking** | Connect related content |
| **Content depth** | Comprehensive, not thin |

---

## 6. Anti-Patterns

| ❌ Don't | ✅ Do |
|----------|-------|
| Jargon-heavy copy | Simple, human language |
| Feature dump | Lead with benefits |
| Wall of text | Short paragraphs, bullet points |
| Multiple CTAs competing | One primary CTA per page |
| Vague promises | Specific, measurable claims |

---

> **Remember:** Good copy is invisible — it guides the reader to take action without feeling "sold to." Test everything, trust data over opinions.
